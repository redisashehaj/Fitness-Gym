About Us

Family gym

The X Gym is one of the few family owned gyms in the area. And because we are not part of a large corporation, you will find we offer a more personal approach to our members and guests. Our goal is provide you with a well equipped, clean, comfortable environment to achieve your fitness goals.

Wide variety

We are a complete training facility with a wide variety of aerobic machines, strength training equipment and several programs including Yoga, Zumba and Silver Sneakers.

Childcare center

We also have a Childcare Center so you can focus on your workout while our Childcare Associates focus on keeping your children entertained! In short, everything you need for a great workout experience.

Friendly staff

Our Staff is here to help you and answer any questions you might have. So please give us a call. Or better yet, just stop in and take a tour or join us for a workout or two. We look forward to seeing you soon at The X Gym.
